ID YANGU Briefing

ID YANGU is a transformative mentorship program designed to support teenagers on their journey towards self-discovery and personal growth. It aims to positively influence their lives by providing guidance, fostering resilience, and encouraging identity development. The initiative stands out for its commitment to nurturing the potential of young individuals, preparing them to face life's challenges with confidence.

By focusing on personal growth and identity, ID YANGU plays an essential role in the lives of its participants. It equips them with the tools necessary to navigate the complexities of adolescence. Through mentorship, the program shapes the future of teenagers, instilling in them the confidence and resilience needed to succeed in their personal and academic endeavors.
